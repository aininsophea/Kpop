To be an EXO-L, one must know...

the Fandom Name

Like other K-pop groups, EXO created the fandom name "EXO-L" for their fans. The "L" stands for Love to express the groups care and love towards their fans.

the Chants

Whether an EXO-L is watching EXO perform live or through a laptop screen, they certainly will cheer for their favourite Kpop group through memorising the chants!

Dresscode

There is no consistent dress code for EXO-Ls but it is always fun to dress up according to EXO's theme colour, which is light grey. Occasionally, during concerts or cafe events, EXO-L fanbases would encourage fans to dress up based on a theme and this will determine whether you would get freebies. So, if you want the freebies, do dress up!

Company

SM Entertainment is a entertainment company in South Korea that has been known to produce popular kpop bands/groups locally and internationally. The director, Lee Sooman, created the intially 12-member group.

Concept

EXO's concept is dark and mysterious. It was meant to be intriguing because it was competing with other Kpop bands that often has a "bad boy" concept. Thus, this mysterious, extraterrestrial and dark concept that included superpowers outshined the other groups' concepts at that time in the year 2012.

Members

Intially, the group had 12 members from South Korea and China. However, due to breach of contract, some members left to pursue their solo careers instead.
